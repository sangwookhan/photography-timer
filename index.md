# Photography Timer

<img src="assets/ptimer-icon.png" alt="Photography Timer app icon" width="120">

Photography Timer is an exposure calculator and shooting timer for film and digital photography.

The app helps calculate adjusted shutter times, film reciprocity-corrected exposures, target shutter differences, and shooting timers.

## Film Data & Attribution

Photography Timer includes selected official film reciprocity data derived from publicly available manufacturer publications and source references.

The app stores normalized factual values and source references only. It does not redistribute datasheet PDFs, copied tables, table images, or third-party copyrighted documents.

## Privacy Policy

Photography Timer does not require a user account.

The app is designed for local shooting-time calculation and timer use. User-created custom film data and timer state are stored on the user's device.

The first public release does not require a remote account, remote film database, or support ticket system.

If privacy-related behavior changes in a future release, this page will be updated before that release.
