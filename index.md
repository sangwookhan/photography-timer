## Features

- ND filter exposure calculation
- Film reciprocity correction
- Target shutter support
- Multi-camera shooting timers

## Film Data & Attribution

Photography Timer includes selected official film reciprocity data derived from publicly available manufacturer publications and source references.

The app stores normalized factual values and source references only. It does not redistribute datasheet PDFs, copied tables, table images, or third-party copyrighted documents.

## Privacy Policy

Photography Timer does not require an account.

For the current public release scope:

- The app does not collect personal information.
- The app does not use advertising.
- The app does not sell user data.
- Timer, camera, film, and custom film settings are stored locally on the device unless the user explicitly exports or shares data in a future feature.
