# Photography Timer

Exposure calculator and countdown timer for film and digital photography.

Photography Timer is preparing for its 0.7 public release.

## Features

- ND filter exposure calculation
- Film reciprocity correction
- Target shutter support
- Multi-camera shooting timers

## Film Data

Film data is centered on official data sheets and manufacturer publications, with publicly available references used as supplemental sources where applicable.

Photography Timer stores normalized factual values and source references. It does not redistribute third-party datasheet PDFs or copied source documents.

## Privacy Policy

Photography Timer does not require an account.

For the current 0.7 release scope:

- The app does not collect personal information.
- The app does not use advertising.
- The app does not sell user data.
- Timer, camera, film, and custom film settings are stored locally on the device unless the user explicitly exports or shares data in a future feature.
